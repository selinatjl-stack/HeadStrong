i. What HeadStrong is

We start a layer deeper than the behaviour.

Most resources for parents treat behaviour as the problem to solve. HeadStrong starts with what's driving the behaviour — the nervous system, the wiring, the gap between what school sees and what's actually happening at home.

The science does the heavy lifting underneath. The language stays warm. You won't find a behaviour chart anywhere on this site.

One through-line connects everything: resilience is a skill, not a personality trait. It develops through the right kind of challenge, with adults who can frame difficulty without removing it. That's what we're building — in you, and in them.
